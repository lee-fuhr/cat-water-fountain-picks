Best cat fountains for 2026

Quick answer

The Catit Flower Fountain is our top pick for its reliable filtration and cat-friendly design that encourages drinking.

The PetSafe Drinkwell 360 is best for owners who want a large stainless steel fountain, while the Veken is a solid budget option and the Catit Pixi is ideal for tiny homes.

Cats evolved to get moisture from prey, so many don’t drink enough from a bowl. A circulating fountain appeals to their instinct for running water, which can boost hydration and reduce the risk of urinary issues. The key is finding one that’s easy to clean, quiet enough for your home, and made from durable materials. When choosing, consider material: stainless steel and ceramic resist bacteria better than plastic but are heavier. Capacity matters for multi-cat households, and filter type affects water quality and maintenance. Noise level and how easy the fountain is to disassemble are also critical for long-term satisfaction.
